About Kaboma Dancers

Preserving and sharing Kenya's vibrant cultural heritage through dance

Our Story

Kaboma Dancers was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to preserve, promote, and share the rich cultural dance traditions of Kenya with the world.

Our journey began with a group of passionate dancers who recognized the importance of keeping traditional Kenyan dances alive while also embracing modern influences. Today, we have grown into one of Kenya's most respected dance companies, performing at venues across the country and internationally.

We take pride in showcasing the diversity of Kenyan culture through our performances, featuring dances from various ethnic groups including the Maasai, Kikuyu, Luhya, Kalenjin, and many others. Each performance tells a story of heritage, community, and celebration.

What Drives Us

The heartbeat of Kaboma Dancers

Our Essence

We exist to keep Kenya's ancestral rhythms alive—bridging generations through movement, rhythm, and storytelling that honors our forefathers while inspiring the future.

The Dream

To place Kenyan dance on the world stage—becoming ambassadors of our rich heritage, one performance at a time, from Nairobi to New York.

Our Promise

Every step tells a story. Every performance celebrates community. We deliver authenticity, passion, and cultural pride in every show.
