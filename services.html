Types of Dance We Offer

Traditional Tribal Dances

Authentic dances from Kenya's various ethnic groups including Maasai Adumu, Kikuyu traditional dances, Luhya Isukuti, and more.

Contemporary African

Modern African dance fusions that blend traditional movements with contemporary choreography.

Drum & Dance

Energetic performances featuring accompanied by synchronized traditional drumming dance movements.

Cultural Storytelling

Performances that tell stories of Kenyan history, legends, and cultural traditions through dance.
